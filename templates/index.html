About

KiloLens is a research tool that uses AI to help you explore Hawaiian-language materials more easily. It supports text, images, documents, and audio, making it easier to find and understand Hawaiian history and knowledge.

Why Hawaiian Research?

Hawaiian-language sources hold generations of insight, like history, politics, and general worldview. Conducting research with Hawaiian materials helps to preserve culture and deepen our understanding of the world.

Features

  • Multilingual support (English or Hawaiian)
  • Multimodal support (text, images, audio, and documents)
  • Advanced AI-powered search and summarization
  • Smart search algorthms to find relevant materials
  • Emphasis on data accuracy and cultural context
  • PDF export [Under construction]
  • Full citation support (APA, Chicago, MLA, and BibTeX) [Under construction]
